about

I found advertising because I was bored out of my mind in law school. I am still in it because I love writing, selling, producing great work. I also am very fond of developing young talent. Throughout my career I had the privilege of working with some of the very best minds in the business. I also have been lucky to win a number of awards. I credit the people I have worked with over the years to making all of that possible. When I’m not at work I enjoy spending time at home with my beautiful wife and our three amazing boys. If we are not at home, we are probably watching them play hockey at rinks that are way too cold.
